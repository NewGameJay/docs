## About New Game+

New Game+ is redefining influencer marketing for modern creators and brands, taking the proven models of traditional creator platforms and restructuring to meet the specific needs of blockchain-based projects.

#### Our Mission
To amplify the vision + communicate the passion of our partners to the **right** audience. 


We're an influencer marketing agency at our core, and through New Game+, we offer a self-service platform that automates campaign management, creator matching, analytics, and more.
