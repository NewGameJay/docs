Our expertise lies in robust content strategies, immersive audience experiences, and impactful creator campaigns. 

Partnering with New Game+ means partnering with a team that takes the time to understand your vision and passion to effectively communicate it to a mass audience.

## What To Expect

An all-in-one advisory team of industry experts to set organizational goals, identify core audiences, and develop key messaging that engages the right audience.

Clear and timely communication is a staple of New Game+ partnerships. Using our in-house application, game partners will get live updates and daily recaps of campaign activity.


## Our Process
<Steps>
  <Step title="Gather Information">
  At the start of a client-agency collaboration, we embark on a thorough information gathering process to lay a strategic foundation, aligning client objectives with target audience insights, competitive analysis, and brand values.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Strategy Development">
  Our team expertly crafts a tailored strategy proposal and carefully selects creators that align with your objectives.  
  </Step>
  <Step title="Feedback + Detailing">
  After presenting the high-level proposal, we eagerly await your feedback on the strategy and creator selection, confirming key messaging is being amplified to the right audience. New Game+ then makes necessary adjustments before commencing the campaign execution. 
  </Step>
  <Step title="Negotiating + Contracting">
  During the negotiation and contracting phase, we proactively engage with agencies and suppliers to secure favorable terms and legally sound agreements, ensuring your peace of mind.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Campaign Execution">
  After contracting, New Game+ swiftly coordinates content and event execution. Our team, in collaboration with your studio, actively supports the content and boosts the creators' audience connection.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Analytics Reporting">
  At the culmination of a successful campaign, we deliver an in-depth analytics report that unveils the impact of both sponsored and organically generated content, highlighting the campaign's success.
  </Step>
</Steps>
