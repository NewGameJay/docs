---
title: 'Reputation'
description: 'Build your trust with partners'
---

Reputation will be built over time, after completing campaigns through New Game+. Verified partners will have the ability to rate each other on a 5-star scale.


On the Reputation page, users can review the following features:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Past Campaigns">
  Users can see past campaigns to verify activity of Creators and Studios. Past Campaigns will share the high level details of who they worked with, the timeline, and the ratings that came from that campaign.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Rating">
  Ratings will be based on a 5-star rating scale. Creators and Studios can rate their experience working together and provide private feedback to improve future campaigns.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Recent Reviews">
  Creators and Studios can leave public reviews for future potential partners to consider when accepting or declining Offers. These reviews can only be submitted by verified partners.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
